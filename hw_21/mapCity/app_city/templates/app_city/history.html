History of the emergence of Yekaterinburg
The history of Yekaterinburg dates back to 1723,
when a fortress factory was founded on the Iset River.
The site for the plant was chosen by Vasily Tatishchev,
the construction of the largest ironworks in Europe was completed
under the leadership of Wilhelm de Gennin.
The city is named after Empress Catherine I.
